# Insta360 X3 Single Lens Picture Settings
>Note it is best to take videos with insta 360 in 360 view mode
> 
This guide gives simple step-by-step settings for taking better **Single Lens photos** on the Insta360 X3.

---

# Option 1: Wide / Outdoor Picture

Use this when you want scenery, family events, travel, backyard, park, beach, or group pictures.

## Steps

1. **Turn on the Insta360 X3**

2. On the main screen, tap the **Switch Lens icon** at the lower-right corner.

3. Select **Single Lens Mode**

4. Change shooting mode to **Photo**

5. Choose the lens view as **Wide**

6. Open settings by **swiping left from the right edge** of the screen.

7. Set:

   **Exposure:** Auto  
   **EV:** 0.0  
   **White Balance:** Auto  
   **Color:** Standard  
   **Timer:** 3 sec, optional but recommended  

8. Hold the camera slightly away from your body.

9. Keep the camera straight.

10. Take the picture.

## Best Simple Outdoor Setting

**Single Lens → Photo → Wide → Auto Exposure → EV 0.0 → WB Auto → Standard → 3-sec timer**

---

# Option 2: Linear / People or Indoor Picture

Use this for people, indoor events, parties, temples, classrooms, restaurants, or normal phone-style pictures.

## Steps

1. **Turn on the Insta360 X3**

2. Tap the **Switch Lens icon** at the lower-right corner.

3. Select **Single Lens Mode**

4. Change shooting mode to **Photo**

5. Choose the lens view as **Linear**

6. Swipe left from the right edge to open settings.

7. Set:

   **Exposure:** Auto  
   **EV:** 0.0  
   **White Balance:** Auto  
   **Color:** Standard  
   **Timer:** 3 sec, if people are posing  

8. Stand a little farther back from people.

9. Keep faces near the center of the frame.

10. Take the picture.

## Best Simple Indoor / People Setting

**Single Lens → Photo → Linear → Auto Exposure → EV 0.0 → WB Auto → Standard → 3-sec timer**

---

# Quick Rule

Use **Wide** when you want to capture **more background**.

Use **Linear** when you want people to look **more natural and less curved**.

## My Recommendation

**Outdoor / group / travel:** Wide  

**Indoor / people / family portraits:** Linear
