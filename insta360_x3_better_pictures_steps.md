# Insta360 X3: Steps to Take Better Pictures

## Best Simple Setting

Use this setting for most family events, travel, and outdoor photos:

**360 Photo → 72MP → PureShot → 3-second timer**

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Turn on the Insta360 X3**

2. **Choose 360 Mode**  
   Use 360 mode so both lenses capture the full scene.

3. **Select Photo Mode**  
   Swipe or tap until you are in **Photo** mode.

4. **Set Photo Resolution to 72MP**  
   This gives the sharpest and most detailed 360 photo.

5. **Turn on PureShot**  
   PureShot improves detail, color, and image quality.
> On the camera screen, swipe left from the right side.
> This opens the Settings page. Tap Photo Format in the lower-left corner. 
> Select PureShot.

6. **Use a 3-second timer**  
   This helps avoid camera shake when pressing the shutter button.

7. **Use an invisible selfie stick or tripod**  
   This gives a better angle and keeps your hand from looking too large in the photo.

8. **Hold the camera slightly above head level**  
   A higher angle usually makes the picture look cleaner and more professional.

9. **Keep the camera straight**  
   Avoid tilting too much unless you want a creative angle.

10. **Avoid placing faces on the lens seam**  
    Do not keep important people or objects exactly on the side edge between the two lenses.

11. **Shoot in good light**  
    Outdoor daylight or bright indoor lighting gives the best picture quality.

12. **Stand slightly away from the camera**  
    If the camera is too close, faces and hands may look distorted.

13. **Edit after taking the photo**  
    In the Insta360 app, use:
    - Reframe
    - Auto color
    - Brightness adjustment
    - Crop or export as a normal photo

---

## When to Use the 3-Second Timer

Use the timer when:
- You are holding the camera by hand
- You are using a selfie stick
- You want a sharper photo
- You are taking a group or family photo

You can skip the timer when:
- You are using the phone app as a remote
- The camera is on a tripod
- You need to take quick photos
- You are using voice control

---

## Best Recommendation

For most situations, use:

**360 Photo → 72MP → PureShot → 3-second timer → invisible selfie stick**

This is the easiest setup for excellent Insta360 X3 pictures.
